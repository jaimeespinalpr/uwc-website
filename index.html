USA Wrestling Affiliation

Official USA Wrestling club

United Wrestling Club participates within the USA Wrestling membership system and sanctioned club structure.

UWC is an affiliated USA Wrestling club. USA Wrestling membership guidance states that all members of a chartered club must be individual members of USA Wrestling.

This helps keep athlete records current and supports sanctioned-club participation standards.

Required before first practice

  • Create or renew the athlete membership through USA Wrestling Membership.
  • Use the athlete profile name that matches your UWC registration.
  • Keep membership active while training in the club.
