Who We Are

Building better wrestlers and better people

UWC uses wrestling as a vehicle for growth, discipline, and confidence that carries beyond the mat.

What we are building

United WC is building a training environment and culture that helps athletes improve in wrestling and in life. We want athletes to grow through the process, develop strong habits, and enjoy the work required to get better.

Mission: Use wrestling as a vehicle to help athletes grow and succeed in any endeavor.

Creed: Better United.

What guides our program

  • Process over outcomes
  • High standards with a positive environment
