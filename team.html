Who We Are

Mission, culture, and coaching philosophy

The program is built to develop athletes through a process, not just chase short-term outcomes.

What we are building

United WC is a wrestling club committed to building an environment and culture that empowers athletes to become better in wrestling and better in life.

We aim to build camaraderie, accountability, and confidence in a room where athletes can succeed on their own merits, fail without judgment, and still enjoy the process.

Mission: Use wrestling as a vehicle to foster growth so athletes can succeed in any endeavor.

Culture: Respect and humility.

Core Values: Resilience, Perseverance, Authenticity.

Creed: It's Bigger Than Wrestling.

Our coaching philosophy

We take a holistic, athlete-centered approach. Technical development matters, but long-term growth starts with relationships, trust, and a consistent process.

  • Everything starts with relationships
  • Adopt a growth mindset
  • Be better today than yesterday
  • Use wrestling to build life skills beyond the mat

How we define success: the willingness, commitment, and perseverance to follow a process that leads to achievement. Success is not just a result; it is a process.

Program objectives

  • Develop a love for the sport
  • Develop a creative and strong mind
  • Develop a solid technical foundation
  • Apply lessons learned to all aspects of life

Athlete expectations

  • Be respectful, humble, and prepared to embrace the process of improvement
  • Represent yourself, your family, and the club with dignity and honor
  • Pursue mastery with purpose, diligence, and discipline
  • Have fun and find joy in the sport

Parent expectations

  • Provide support, encouragement, and perspective throughout the season
  • Be proud of your wrestler for taking on a demanding sport
  • Model good sportsmanship and positive support for wrestlers, coaches, and officials
  • Support the process of getting better, not only outcomes or short-term rewards
